Keys Please® has been operating since 1997. Whether it is a medical appointment, a wedding, a corporate party or a night on the town our teams of professional drivers will transport you in your vehicle, along with any guests that fit safely in your vehicle, to the destination you require. Or, we will deliver just your vehicle to the desired location. Stampede Party? Flames Game? Watching the Stampeders? We are available 365 days a year. Call, text or download our app to book your designated driver.
